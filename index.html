More About Me

Greetings! My name is Jiaxuan Li

I am a second year master student (graduate in January 2020) in Computer Science at University of Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China, under the supervision of Professor philippe fournier-viger. I got my bachelor degree from Hainan University in 2017.

Research Interest

My current research lies in data mining, specifically high utility pattern mining.

For PHD, I am interested in the intersection of DM with NLP, ML or DL to discover valuable and new knowledge to facilitate users' decision and reinforce the models of interpretability.
